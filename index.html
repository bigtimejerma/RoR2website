RISK OF RAIN 2

Pick your survivor. Collect items. Survive the deluge of danger in an alien world.

FEEL THE RUSH OF INTENSE, ABILITY-DRIVEN GAMEPLAY

Risk of Rain 2 is all about picking a character that feels right to you. Each one wields unique abilities.

Create a unique item-based build to overcome enemies, hazards, and grand bossfights to continue through stages.

Experience firsthand the principle of Lindley's "why we play" - overcome, adapt, and go wild.

EXPERIENCE AN ALIEN ATMOSPHERE ON THE PLANET OF PETRICHOR V

Fight through the seemingly familiar but unquestionably alien atmospheres of Petrichor V.

With numerous stages possessing different native lifeforms, you're always certain to experience something new.
